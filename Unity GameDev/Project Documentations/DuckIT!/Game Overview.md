# Concept
## General Idea:
The game is a 2D platformer game. It will have a total of 10 levels. Player is faced with the world of glitches where anything can happen, it has to beat the glitch demon and once again set the liberation duck free.
The enemies can change or glitch player mechanics and the level environment. 
Program is a breading ground for a enemy which can corrupt anything that it touches, i.e., these monsters will try to escape the program and infect the computer. More you lose, more infected your computer get. And just like the duck goose virus, the enemies will then start appearing in your system making it a living horror.
Player has to go around deciphering code, find clues and find the enemy that is the cause of glitch in that particular level. Once that particular enemy is drawn out and "killed" by the player, that level becomes glitch free and player can move onto the next level. 

## Genre
Platformer 2D, 2D Shooting, Puzzle

## Unique selling point
The enemies in the game can glitch your system, i.e., the system the actual person is using to play this game. Although it can be fixed by the game, but as you progress, there will be less scope to make mistake, or else you would have only two option, one would be to quit the game, lose all your progress, or the other would be to work in a buggy system until you finish the game.

# Objective
## Player
Player Objective is to get rid of the enemy either by letting it consume the duplicate of the player which would be made due to the consequence of player action, or to let the enemy bug another virtual space,i.e., your system, pass each level and free the liberation duck.

Do not let enemy touch you, the part that gets touched will be bugged, and no longer available to use for the player. By parts, it means legs, hands, head, etc,.
## Enemy
Enemy goal is to bug up everything.
